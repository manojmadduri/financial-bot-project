Our Discord Bot Services

Stock Bot

Access real-time stock market data with simple commands like !stock SYMBOL. Our bot provides current prices, changes, and percentage updates to help you monitor the market.

Crypto Bot

Stay updated with cryptocurrency pricing and market trends using !crypto coin_name. Get the latest data on your favorite digital assets.

Market Alerts

Receive periodic market updates and custom alerts directly in your Discord channels. Configure your alerts to keep you informed about key market changes.

Advanced Analysis

Our advanced analysis feature goes beyond basic data. Using the command !analyze SYMBOL, the bot:

  • Retrieves historical daily stock data from Alpha Vantage.
  • Calculates key technical indicators such as the 20-day and 50-day Simple Moving Averages (SMA).
  • Generates an insightful chart showing closing prices and moving averages.

Example: Try typing !analyze AAPL in your Discord channel to see a detailed chart for Apple Inc. For more details on this feature, visit our Advanced Analysis page.
